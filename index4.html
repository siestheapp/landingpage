FAQ

Do I need to measure myself?
No. Sies learns your fit from the clothes you already own. Snap a couple tag photos or paste product links and we'll extract measurements automatically.
Will this work across different brands?
Yes. We standardize brand size guides and map them to your fit profile, so an XS in one brand can be compared to an S in another.
What if I like different fits?
Choose a fit zone – sleek, perfect, or relaxed – and Sies adapts by garment type. You'll also see a short "why it fits" explanation on each item.
How is my data used?
Only to improve your recommendations. We don't sell personal data. You can remove your info anytime from Settings.
