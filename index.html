Why Sies works

Built on real garments from your closet plus brand-specific size guides. The result: predictions that feel human — and fit.

Closet-aware

Uses what you already own

Reference items become your personal baseline. Sies translates brand jargon into comparable measurements.

Brand-specific

Size guides are standardized and mapped, so an XS in Brand A can be compared to an S in Brand B.

Contextual

Tight, perfect, or relaxed

Choose a fit zone and see how each size will actually feel — not just a number range.
