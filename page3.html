FAQ

Do I need to measure myself?
No. Sies learns your fit from the clothes you already own.
Will this work across different brands?
Yes. We standardize brand size guides and map them to your fit profile.
What if I like different fits?
Choose a fit zone – sleek, perfect, or relaxed – and Sies adapts by garment type.
